#1 RECOMMENDATION

AERATION

  • It is recommended that you Aerate your lawn, except sandy soil. This helps increase the penetration of both water and fertilizers to the root zone and reduce susceptibility to disease.
  • Aeration is not a one time fix, but rather part of a cultural lawn care practice your lawn benefits from over time.
ALSO AVAILABLE IN PROGRAM 3

ECO CLEAR

  • Used for driveway edges, sidewalk edges that grow grass or weeds.
  • Not for lawns.
ALSO AVAILABLE IN PROGRAM 3

CRABGRASS MANAGEMENT

  • Product that helps control germination of weeds, crabgrass, and wild grasses over time.

MICRONUTRIENTS

  • Soil conditioner. A chelated mixture containing necessary turf nutrients maximizing your lawn’s potential.
  • Great for stressed lawns in the summer.

LIMING

  • Soil conditioner. Helps balance acidic soils, stimulates microbial activity in soil.
  • Well paired with Program 2.
